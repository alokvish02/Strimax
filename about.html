About Us

Background and Experience of Mr. Ashok Chavan

Mr. Ashok Chavan, the Managing Director of Sterimax, is a Technocrat and award-winning entrepreneur with over 20 years of experience in the Blow Fill Seal (BFS) and Form Fill Seal (FFS) machine technology. He is a graduate engineer who founded Sterimax in November 2007 with the vision of providing innovative machinery to the Indian pharmaceutical industry.

Addressing Market Gaps

When Indian manufacturers struggled to meet the demand for IV fluid bottles and Water for Injection (WFI) due to the high cost of BFS/FFS machines and molds, which ran into hundreds of millions of rupees, Mr. Chavan recognized the gap and took initiative to provide affordable, high-quality solutions for the pharmaceutical sector.

Sterimax's Growth and Success

Under his leadership, Sterimax established a state-of-the-art manufacturing unit in Mahape, Navi Mumbai, equipped with over 20 imported VMC machines. The company has grown into one of the leading manufacturers of BFS/FFS machines in India, serving the pharmaceutical packaging market with a strong technical, marketing, and service team.
